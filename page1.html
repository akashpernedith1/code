Ryan Potter is an American actor. He made his acting debut as the lead of the Nickelodeon series Supah Ninjas. In animation, he voiced Hiro Hamada in the animated film Big Hero 6 and its television series sequel. Potter also currently portrays Garfield "Gar" Logan / Beast Boy in the series Titans.









Robert Scott Adsit is an American actor, comedian and writer. Born on the North Shore of Illinois, Adsit joined the mainstage cast of Chicago's The Second City in 1994 after attending Columbia College Chicago.









Damon Kyle Wayans Jr. is an American actor and comedian most widely known for starring as Brad Williams in the ABC sitcom Happy Endings for which he was nominated for the Critics' Choice Television Award for Best Supporting Actor in a Comedy Series in 2012 and as Coach in the Fox sitcom New Girl.
